RULES
Non-Negotiables

The Rules
Don't Bend

01
Risk Is Defined Before Entry
Every trade has a hard stop defined before the order is placed. Never enter a trade we can't afford to lose. Risk is a fixed number, not a feeling.
02
Stops Are Respected — Always
Moving a stop against your position is not risk management. It's hope. Stops get hit. That's part of the game. Don't negotiate with the market.
03
Process Comes Before Outcome
A losing trade executed correctly beats a winning trade executed sloppily. We judge the decision, not the result. If the process was right, we move forward.
04
No Chasing, No Revenge
Missed your entry? It's gone. Had a bad morning? That's done too. Every trade lives in isolation. Emotional carry-over is how accounts blow up.
